About Beck’s Depression Inventory

The Beck Depression Inventory (BDI) is a 21-item self-reporting questionnaire for evaluating the severity of depression. Its result is an indication, not a diagnosis. If the result suggests depressive symptoms, you should speak to a mental health professional.

Depression is a serious illness that requires treatment. If left untreated, it can worsen and even endanger your life.
